# docs/landscapes/

Integrated maps across the corpus — the level above thematic syntheses. Where a synthesis answers "what does the
corpus say about X?", a landscape answers "how do X, Y, and Z relate, and where does each fit against Linus's
architecture and roadmap?"

## Active documents

- [`total-landscape.md`](total-landscape.md) — the master integration map. Crosses all 27 syntheses against
  architecture, roadmap phases, and the open-questions backlog. The right entry point for a newcomer who wants one
  document covering everything.
- [`synthesis-landscape.md`](synthesis-landscape.md) — cross-synthesis structural map showing overlaps, tensions, and
  hub clusters across the 15 thematic + 12 cluster syntheses. Best read after you know two or three syntheses well and
  want to see how the corpus stitches together.

## Deprecated (kept for link integrity)

- [`paper-landscape.md`](paper-landscape.md) — predecessor of the synthesis layer; navigation function moved to
  [`../paper-notes/INDEX.md`](../paper-notes/INDEX.md).
- [`repo-landscape.md`](repo-landscape.md) — same shape, predecessor for the repo-notes layer.

Both are retained as stubs that point downstream so existing cross-links keep resolving.
